Build a full-stack SaaS web application called:

"AMTS Pro – Multi-Strategy Trading Intelligence Platform"

---

🎯 CORE PURPOSE:

A modular trading intelligence platform that provides BUY / SELL / WAIT signals based on multiple trading strategies.

The system must NOT blindly combine all strategies. Instead:

- Each strategy generates its own signal
- A scoring system ranks signals
- The system outputs the highest-confidence signal

No auto trading. Signals only.

---

🧠 STRATEGY ENGINE ARCHITECTURE:

The app must support 6 PRIMARY STRATEGY CATEGORIES:

---

1. TREND TRADING

Logic:

- Trade in direction of trend

Strategies:

- EMA 50 / EMA 200 crossover
- Moving average trend following

Rules:

- Uptrend → BUY only
- Downtrend → SELL only

---

2. RANGE TRADING

Logic:

- Buy at support, sell at resistance

Strategies:

- Support & Resistance zones
- Horizontal range trading

Rules:

- Near support → BUY
- Near resistance → SELL

---

3. MOMENTUM / RSI TRADING

Use:

- RSI indicator

Strategies:

- Overbought / Oversold
- Mean reversion

Rules:

- RSI < 30 → BUY
- RSI > 70 → SELL

---

4. BREAKOUT TRADING

Logic:

- Trade when price breaks key levels

Strategies:

- Resistance breakout
- Break and hold strategy

Rules:

- Break above resistance + volume → BUY
- Break below support → SELL

---

5. SCALPING STRATEGY

Logic:

- Fast trades, small profits

Strategies:

- 1-minute / 5-minute quick entries
- Micro trend trades

Rules:

- Quick entry/exit signals
- High frequency signals

---

6. SWING + EVENT TRADING

Logic:

- Hold trades longer or trade major events

Strategies:

- Fibonacci retracement
- News trading (CPI, interest rates, earnings)

---

📊 ADDITIONAL STRATEGY MODULES:

The system must also support these sub-strategies:

PRICE ACTION:

- Support & resistance
- Candlestick patterns
- Chart patterns

SMART MONEY CONCEPT (ICT):

SUPPLY & DEMAND:

🧠 SIGNAL ENGINE DESIGN:

Each strategy must:

1. Run independently
2. Output:

{
strategy_name: "",
signal: "BUY" | "SELL" | "WAIT",
confidence_score: 0–100,
entry: number,
stop_loss: number,
take_profit: number
}

---

📊 SIGNAL AGGREGATION SYSTEM:

- Combine all strategy outputs
- Rank by confidence score
- Display:

1. Top Signal (highest confidence)
2. Supporting signals
3. Conflicting signals warning
